Frequently Asked Questions

What are the types of platform that Hyperio creates?

Hyperio creates a variety of software engineering services including IoT platform development, web and mobile applications as well as cloud backend (API) interfaces.

  • Hyperio develops mobile applications for the Android ecosystem - these are applications that run on mobile devices such as tablets and smartphones (see the usecase 1)
  • We also create web applications (web apps). They are running in a web browser and can be used by a computer, tablet or smartphone.
  • IoT platforms are solutions that involve connected devices (IoT devices) such as smart sensors, remote home automation solutions or data collections platforms.
  • Enterprise platform consists of complex backends, distributed databases and API solutions - enabling large organization complex business process management (BPM), resource planning (ERP) and various customer relationship management (CRM)
